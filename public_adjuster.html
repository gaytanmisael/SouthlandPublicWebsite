Why use a Public Adjuster?

We are Professional adjusters who will assist you the policy holder in the presentation, adjustment and settlement of your claim. The insurance company's adjuster has a primary duty to act in the best interest of their employer, not yours! As your representative, we have both an obligation and duty to contest the insurance company on your behalf. "We in effect level the playing field!". The policy of insurance states condition that if no met may impair your ability to Recover rightful benefits. We take our clients step by step through the adjustment process insuring our clients in full compliance. We pride ourselves in great customer service and you can be assured that your claim will be handled with utmost care.

We in effect level the playing field!
